How Does Pro Air Duct Cleaning Conduct Air Duct Cleaning?

Our air duct cleaning process, known as source removal, stands as the most effective method to thoroughly cleanse your duct system. This process involves two key steps:

  1. Agitation to dislodge debris
  2. Debris removal using specialized equipment

Our technicians employ high-pressure air whips and directional attachments to dislodge dirt and debris, while a powerful Abatement Technologies HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) filtered vacuum captures these contaminants. HEPA filters excel in trapping particles as minuscule as 0.3 microns, ensuring the efficient removal of microscopic allergens, dust mites, pet dander, pollen, and other airborne pollutants. This prevents their recirculation into your home's air.

This technology ensures a thorough cleaning, targeting not just visible debris but also hidden contaminants that can jeopardize indoor air quality and contribute to health issues.

Is Air Duct Cleaning Essential?

Cleaning your residential ductwork effectively removes accumulated dust, debris, and even rodent droppings, facilitating smoother airflow and enhancing system efficiency. Keep an eye out for these signs indicating the need for professional cleaning:

  • Visible dust, cobwebs, or debris blowing out of air vents
  • Presence of rodent droppings or insects inside ductwork
  • Inadequate airflow

Remember, air duct cleaning isn’t a one-time task. The frequency of cleaning depends on factors such as home ownership status, AC system maintenance, air filter changes, and pet ownership.
