Problems with Leaky Ductwork

Leaky ducts can lead to significant energy losses, uneven heating, increased humidity, and unnecessary strain on your HVAC system. Neglecting duct maintenance can jeopardize your family's comfort and result in higher energy bills.

The Aeroseal Process

The Aeroseal duct sealing process involves:

  • Thorough inspection of the HVAC system to identify leaks
  • Cleaning the ducts to remove debris and optimize sealing efficiency
  • Introduction of aerosol particles into the ducts to seal leaks effectively
  • Monitoring and verification of the sealing process

The Aeroseal Duct Sealing Process

The Aeroseal duct sealing process is a highly effective method for sealing leaks in air duct systems. It offers a convenient, non-invasive alternative to traditional sealing methods and ensures improved energy efficiency and indoor air quality. The process includes:

  • Initial inspection and assessment of ductwork
  • Cleaning to remove debris and contaminants
  • Sealing using aerosol particles and pressure to fill leaks
  • Monitoring and verification of the sealing effectiveness
  • Final inspection to ensure all leaks are properly sealed
